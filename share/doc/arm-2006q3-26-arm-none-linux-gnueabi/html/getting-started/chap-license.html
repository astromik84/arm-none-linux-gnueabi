Chapter 1. ARM Sourcery G++ Lite Licenses

Abstract

ARM Sourcery G++ Lite contains software provided under a variety of licenses. Some components are "free" or "open source" software, while other components are proprietary. This chapter explains what licenses apply to your use of ARM Sourcery G++ Lite. You should read this chapter to understand your legal rights and obligations as a user of ARM Sourcery G++ Lite.
